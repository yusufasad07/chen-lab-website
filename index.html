What we do

Transforming cardiovascular care through engineered biomaterials

Our interdisciplinary team bridges materials science, mechanics, and cell biology to design responsive implants, smart bioadhesives, and patient-specific therapies.

Resorbable Metallo-Elastomer Vascular Grafts

Developing mechanically robust, bioresorbable grafts that adapt to pulsatile blood flow while promoting long-term vessel remodeling.

Explore research →

Piezoelectric Modulation of Endothelialization

Harnessing mechanical-electrical coupling to direct vascular cell behavior and accelerate endothelial coverage on implants.

3D-Printed Cardiac Patches

Engineering microchanneled scaffolds that deliver oxygen, cues, and therapeutic cells to damaged myocardium.

Lab Snapshot

Collaborative science with clinical impact
